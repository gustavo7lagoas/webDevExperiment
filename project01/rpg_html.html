What is RPG

History

Forget about all those video games. That was not the way it started. Firstly we have the boardgames where battles between fictional armies happened and there was a winner. One day, someone have the idea of being one of the armies soldiers instead being the army commander. And after that people started to get together each one representing only one character

Variants

Type Characteristics
Pen and Paper More traditional style some one narrates and the others take action
Live Action Improvisational game. People dressed and "are" the character
Video Game Some of the interpretation part was lost here but it still a lot of fun

Some titles

  • Fantasy: Dungeons and Dragons
  • Generic: GURPS
  • Personal horror: Storyteller
